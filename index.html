Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes! This website is completely free to use. You can learn vocabulary, practice, and improve your English without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account? yes
Yes, you need to create an account to save your progress, track your learned words, and get a better learning experience.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by learning a few new words every day, using them in sentences, reading English books or articles, watching English movies or videos, and practicing with friends or online. The key is learn → use → review regularly.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, after finishing a course you will receive a certificate that shows your achievement in learning English vocabulary.
